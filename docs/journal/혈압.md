- **수축기혈압(최고혈압, SYStolic pressure)**
  - 90 ~ 120 mmHg
- **이완기혈압(최저혈압, DIAstolic pressure)**
  - 60 ~ 80 mmHg

| BLOOD PRESSURE CATEGORY                                                                                                                                                                                                        | SYSTOLIC mm Hg (upper number) | and/or | DIASTOLIC mm Hg (lower number) |
